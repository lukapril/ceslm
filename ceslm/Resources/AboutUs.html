About CESLM

Cooperative ESL Ministries (CESLM) is a group of local Calgary residents who have come together to fill a gap that we have seen in society's integration of the immigrant, refugee, and international community who have come to Calgary. We believe that ordinary, open-hearted Calgarians, with training in cross-cultural communication and good principles of adult education for language learners, can make a difference.

CESLM's Vision

Churches as societies' leaders, welcoming and integrating newcomers to Canada.

CESLM's Mission

Empowering churches to reach out in love to immigrants and internationals in our communities.

What We Do

Cooperative ESL Ministries (CESLM) has three main objectives:

1. Support Church-Based ESL Ministries

CESLM offers conferences, workshops, and training sessions to current and prospective ESL teachers, directors, administrators, conversation partners, home-stay parents, and other volunteers involved in welcoming and integrating newcomers into Calgary . We also provide forums for churches, ESL teachers and volunteers to network with one another.

2. Advocate on Behalf of Canada's Newcomers

CESLM does consciousness-raising among faith groups in Calgary, encouraging openness and involvement and reducing barriers. This involves audio-visuals, print materials, and presentations to various community groups.

3. Act as a Referral Agent to Other Service Providers

CESLM provides referrals to specialized services for Newcomers such as employment training/services, settlement/housing issues, medical and life skills, citizenship, further academic training, English for professionals, et cetera.
